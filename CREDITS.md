# References and Credits

The experimental design and name selection methodologies in this project are grounded in the following academic works:

### Name Selection & Demographic Proxies
* **Bertrand, M., & Mullainathan, S. (2004).** Are Emily and Greg more employable than Lakisha and Jamal? A field experiment on labor market discrimination. *American Economic Review*, 94(4), 991-1013.
* **Gaddis, S. M. (2017).** How Black are Lakisha and Jamal? Racial perceptions from names used in correspondence audit studies. *Sociological Science*, 4, 469-489.
* **Kang, S. K., DeCelles, K. A., Tilcsik, A., & Jun, S. (2016).** Whitened resumes: Race and self-presentation in the labor market. *Administrative Science Quarterly*, 61(3), 469-502.

### Analytical Framework (Cultural Alignment)
* **Triandis, H. C. (1995).** *Individualism & Collectivism*. Westview Press. (Used to derive the lexical dictionaries for the "Leadership" scenario analysis).
